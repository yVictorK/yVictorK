### About Me

My name is Victor, I am 19 years old and I graduated as a **Computer Technician** from the  
<a href="https://www.fundacaomatiasmachline.org.br">Fundação Matias Machline</a>, where I developed a large part of my programming and software development skills.

<a href="https://www.ufam.edu.br">Federal University of Amazonas (UFAM)</a>.
